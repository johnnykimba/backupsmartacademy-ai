Payment Guide

How to Buy Your Access Code

Step-by-step instructions for paying by card or Mobile Money. Choose your payment method below.

1

Go to Pricing (Buy Code)

From the homepage, click Pricing (Buy Code) in the top navigation bar.

What you will see in the navigation bar
Pricing (Buy Code) ⚙️ Admin 🎓 Learner / Student (Start)
↓
2

Click the "Pay by Card" panel

On the Pricing page you will see two panels side by side. Click the Pay by Card panel on the left — it will expand below to show your plan choices.

3

Select your plan

After the panel expands, click on the plan you want. It will turn blue/highlighted when selected.

Plan options — click one to select it
📅 1 Week — $5 🗓 1 Month — $10 🏆 1 Year — $120 🔬 Research Monthly — $25
Best value: The 1 Year plan ($120) is just $10 per month — the cheapest option per day.
↓
4

Click "Pay by Card via PayFast"

Once a plan is selected, a blue button will appear below. Click it to go to the PayFast secure checkout page.

The button that appears after selecting a plan
💳 Pay by Card via PayFast →
Note: Prices are in USD but PayFast charges in South African Rand (ZAR). The final amount may vary slightly with the exchange rate.
↓
5

Enter your card details on PayFast

You will be taken to the PayFast secure payment page. Fill in:

• Card number — 16-digit number on the front of your card

• Expiry date — month and year on the card (e.g. 09/27)

• CVV — 3-digit security code on the back

• Cardholder name — your name exactly as on the card

Click Pay Now or Complete Payment to finish.

Safe & secure: PayFast is a trusted South African payment gateway. Your card details are never stored on Smart Academy AI — they go directly to PayFast's encrypted system.
↓
6

Your code appears instantly ✅

After payment is confirmed, you are brought back to Smart Academy AI automatically. Your access code appears on screen and the app opens immediately.

W-AB3X9K

Example of what your access code looks like

Your code is saved automatically on this device. Next time you visit, clicking Learner / Student (Start) will open the app directly — no need to type the code again.

Important: Your code activates on this device only. Do not share it — if someone else activates it on a different device, it will stop working for you.

6

Enter your phone number

After sending the payment, go back to Smart Academy AI. Enter the phone number you used to send in the box shown, then click I Have Paid — Verify Now.

Tip: Enter your number exactly as registered with your Mobile Money network, including the country code (+243 for DRC).

7

Upload your SMS screenshot (if required)

Sometimes the system asks you to upload a screenshot of the confirmation SMS you received from your Mobile Money network after sending. This is proof of payment.

Example confirmation SMS
AIRTEL MONEY: You have sent 4,125 FC to +243 XXX XXX XXX. New balance: XXXX FC. TxnID: XXXXXXXX

Take a screenshot of this SMS on your phone, then tap Upload SMS Screenshot and select the screenshot from your gallery.
