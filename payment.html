Note: Prices are in USD but PayFast charges in South African Rand (ZAR). The final amount may vary slightly with the exchange rate.
↓
5

Enter your card details on PayFast

You will be taken to the PayFast secure payment page. Fill in your card details and click Pay Now.

• Card number — 16-digit number on the front of your card

• Expiry date — month/year printed on the card (e.g. 09/27)

• CVV — 3-digit code on the back of the card

• Name on card — your name exactly as it appears

Safe & secure: PayFast is a trusted South African payment gateway. Your card details are never stored on Smart Academy AI.
↓
6

Your access code appears instantly ✅

After payment is confirmed, you are brought back to Smart Academy AI automatically. Your access code appears on screen and the app opens immediately.

W-AB3X9K

Example of what your access code looks like

Your code is saved automatically on this device. Next time you click Learner / Student (Start), the app opens directly — no need to type the code again.

Important: Your code activates on this device only. Do not share it with others.

6

Copy your proof of payment and paste it here

After sending the payment, your Mobile Money network sends you a confirmation SMS. Copy the text of that SMS and paste it into the proof-of-payment box on the Smart Academy AI screen.

Example confirmation SMS — copy this entire text
AIRTEL MONEY: You have sent 4,125 FC to +243 XXX XXX XXX. Your new balance is XXXX FC. TxnID: XXXXXXXX
Then paste it into this box on the app:
Paste your SMS proof of payment here...
Tip: Press and hold your SMS message to copy the full text, then paste it into the box on the app screen.
↓
7

Click "Submit" and get your code

After pasting your proof of payment, click the Submit button. The system will process your payment and deliver your access code.
